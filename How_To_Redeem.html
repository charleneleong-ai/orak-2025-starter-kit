Brev Best Practices

Please Note: Brev instances are pay-by-the-hour.

This is important to understand to make the most of the credits you’ve been given. Make sure to stop instances when not being used. Any time the instance is running, it is using credits from your account at the typical running rate for your compute.

This warning will be displayed when creating an instance. For the best hackathon experience, pick an instance that can be restarted for maximum value from your credits.
